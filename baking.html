HOW TO GET STARTED
Step 1: Review recipe to ensure that you know what materials/equipment are needed as well as how long it will take.
Step 2: Purchase all necessary materials/equipment.
Step 3: Invite friends over to bake with you/share your treats with!

TIPS TO PRACTICE AND IMPROVE
Research! Have a question, chances are plenty of people have tried to find a solution already.
Practice daily! If it becomes too expensive, cut recipes in half to save money/time.
Watch plenty of inspiring shows/documentaries on baking. My favorite, is The Great British Baking Show.
